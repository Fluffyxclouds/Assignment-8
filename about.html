About Restick

Welcome to Restick — where passion for taste meets modern simplicity.

We started our journey to bring together the best of both worlds — cozy atmosphere and delicious meals. Every dish is made with care, using only fresh and local ingredients. Whether it’s a quick coffee break or a family dinner, Restick is your go-to place for comfort and flavor.

Our chefs believe that good food should be simple yet memorable. That’s why each recipe is crafted with balance and creativity in mind. Come taste the difference — because at Restick, every bite tells a story.
